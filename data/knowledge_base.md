# AutoStream Product Information

## Pricing & Plans

### Basic Plan
- **Cost**: $29/month
- **Features**:
  - 10 videos per month
  - 720p resolution

### Pro Plan
- **Cost**: $79/month
- **Features**:
  - Unlimited videos
  - 4K resolution
  - AI captions

## Company Policies

- **Refund Policy**: No refunds after 7 days.
- **Support**: 24/7 support is available only on the Pro plan.
